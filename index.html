An easy to use learning management system

This is a learning management system, I created recently so that primary, secondary and higher secondary level educational institutions can use this site for reaching further.

This is just a prototype. I would be happy to make it evolved into something better which can be our next step to reaching students from less privileged area. But it would be nicer to have a sponsorship, little marketing to make people know about it.
